Summary of tweets for March 23rd, 2012

by Professor Sample Posted on March 22, 2012

Below is a summary of tweets from March 23rd, 2012

@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 ImmorTall: feels similar to Passage. you cant win, but there are sacrifices you make to do things other better like lose the family – 22 Mar 12 23:55
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 ImmorTall: unfortunately it looks like you can’t win even if you don’t bother protecting the family…I did get further tho i think – 22 Mar 12 23:53
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 OnceUponASpaceTime: The game teaches you to be patient, as there is nothing in this game that goes fast – 22 Mar 12 23:40
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 OnceUponASpacetime: I had no idea what I was doing, and was pretty much just clicking on stuff to light it up… – 22 Mar 12 23:38
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 Unmanned: The multitasking requires some coordination, but other than that, the rest of the game is memorization – 22 Mar 12 23:30
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 Unmanned: some very basic minigames, some of which require you to click while continue doing the minigame, those were interesting – 22 Mar 12 23:29
@Bitter_Requiem: Spent works to build empathy for low-incomers so the player is worked into an empathetic, vulnerable state to donate. Well played. #HNRS353 – 22 Mar 12 23:14
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 Dys4ia: I suppose the game could be used as something of a confidence provider for people going through a sex change…? – 22 Mar 12 23:12
@shandlerO_o: #HNRS353 Dys4ia: the game felt like a series of dull minigames (some of which you didnt even have to finish) and the music seemed not to fit – 22 Mar 12 23:11
@Bitter_Requiem: I liked Spent’s pacing. It’s effective. As your money gets depleted, the pressure gets to you and you make worse decisions faster. #HNRS353 – 22 Mar 12 23:06
@Bitter_Requiem: Stop Disasters! educates the player about using limited monetary resources and knowledge effectively to save lives during disasters #HNRS353 – 22 Mar 12 23:02
@Bitter_Requiem: Stop Disasters! was interesting. Very much a la SimCity, only played with intentions of a less dubious nature. Enjoyed it, actually.#HNRS353 – 22 Mar 12 22:44
@dluc353: #hnrs353 Once upon a Spacetime: makes you feel. Curious, sad, happy, mad. Draws you in to feel, even if u dnt know what’s going on. – 22 Mar 12 22:43
@dluc353: #hnrs353 Once upon a Spacetime: hard to understand. Wanted to initiate interaction with girl, but couldn’t figure it out. Emotion inducing. – 22 Mar 12 22:42
@dluc353: #hnrs353 Spent: gives ppl like me a small taste of the hardships of coming from low-income lifestyle. Easier to see why ppl do what they do. – 22 Mar 12 22:39
