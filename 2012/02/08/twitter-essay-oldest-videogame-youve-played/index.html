Twitter Essay: Oldest Videogame You’ve Played

by Professor Sample Posted on February 8, 2012
#HNRS353 Twitter Essay: What is the oldest game you've played? Title, year of game, and platform. And what is most memorable about the game?

Just a reminder to tweet in a Twitter essay of exactly 140 characters the oldest videogame you’ve ever played outside of this class. Be sure to use the #HNRS353 hashtag to make sure your tweet shows up in our class feed!
