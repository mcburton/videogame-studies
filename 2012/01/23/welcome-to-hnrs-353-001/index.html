Welcome to HNRS 353-001

by Professor Sample Posted on January 23, 2012

Welcome to the course website and blog for HNRS 353-001, an Honors College seminar focusing on the cultural impact and analysis of videogames. Here you’ll find all the course documents, including the most up-to-date version of the schedule as well as the requirements for the course.

If you are a student in HNRS 353, an account to this blog will shortly be created for you.

About Professor Sample

Mark Sample is an Assistant Professor in the Department of English at George Mason University, where he researches and teaches contemporary and experimental literature, electronic literature, graphic novels, and videogames.
